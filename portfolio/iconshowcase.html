These are some icon concepts that i created revolving around traveling and transport.


Before starting the assignment i knew i wanted to keep the art for the icons very simple, due to different reasons. First i wanted to keep them as simplified as i could get them because i knew beforehand i wanted a minimalistic style of look. Second for icons to work properly the graphic needs to be clear no matter the size. Therefore i kept the details to a minimum.


I wanted them to have a modern feel to them, and decided to go with gradients for the background color with a white figure (inspired by logos such as Instagram). The colors are colors that i would describe as fresh and vivid. Combining these into a gradient i feel really helped bring the icons to life, so i could lay low on detailing the figures.
