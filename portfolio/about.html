ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Frederik Frydensbjerg, a student at CPH Business currently studying multimediadesign. I am person who is very passionate and dedicated to creating beautiful designs, and I enjoy every step of the process, from discussion and project management to concept and execution.

In my free time I like creating logos and website designs. Ever since I was around 12 years old, I have always been interested in visual communications in all its forms. It was around that time I first started using photoshop to create effects like flames onto text-objects. Ever since then my hobby and passion for the visual aspect has stuck around.
