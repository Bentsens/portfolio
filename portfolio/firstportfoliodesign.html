In our first major assignment we were given the task of designing our "dream-portfolio".


The requirements for the assigment was as below stated:


  • Create an adobe XD prototype or by using similar prototype tools (figma)

  • Hand in one PDF containing maximum of 3 pages, that describes and document your prototype


For my portfolio i was trying to make "minimalism" and "modern" my keywords. In order to create a portfolio by those guidelines i chose to use very little color. At that point in time i was not in possession of an image of myself, and therefore i used a model as a placeholder for time being
